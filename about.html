What SISU means

SISU is a Finnish concept representing grit, resilience, and quiet strength. It is the discipline to build things the right way, even when the work is hard.

Our philosophy

Data should be clear, reliable, and actionable. We build systems that last, solve real problems, and empower teams to make better decisions.

Founder
