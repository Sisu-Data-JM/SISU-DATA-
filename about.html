What SISU means

SISU is a Finnish concept representing grit, resilience, and quiet strength. It is the discipline to build things the right way, even when the work is hard — an unyielding will to push through extreme adversity.

Our philosophy

Data should be clear, reliable, and actionable. We build systems that last, solve real problems, and empower teams to make better decisions.

Founder

SISU Data was founded by Jared Miettunen, a healthcare data and analytics executive with deep expertise in data engineering, SQL, analytics modernization, product development, and enterprise solution design.

Throughout my career, I've partnered with some of the nation's largest health systems to solve complex data challenges. My focus has always been the same: transform fragmented, manual processes into scalable data platforms that increase organizational capacity, accelerate decision-making, and unlock measurable business value. By helping executive leaders replace slow, error-prone workflows with modern data infrastructure, I've enabled organizations to move faster, reduce risk, and capture opportunities that were previously out of reach.

My experience spans data governance, enterprise architecture, product strategy, and large-scale solution delivery. I led the redesign of a complex due diligence data architecture that reduced turnaround time from four weeks to just 48 hours, creating more than $10 billion in client value. I architected a centralized data infrastructure that expanded a solution design organization from 15 to more than 150 professionals while increasing concurrent client capacity from one implementation to more than ten. I've also led the design, development, testing, and enterprise release of multiple data products and applications. One of the most significant initiatives was leading a full platform migration to a Microsoft-based data exchange, delivering the project two months ahead of schedule, within a $600,000 budget, reducing operating costs by 90%, and generating an 18% annual return on investment.

While each of these accomplishments is different, they all share one common foundation: SISU.

I grew up in the forests of northern Minnesota in a Finnish family where the word sisu wasn't just something we said — it was something we lived. In Finnish culture, sisu represents extraordinary determination, resilience, integrity, and the courage to persevere when the path is difficult. It's the belief that obstacles are meant to be overcome through grit, discipline, and unwavering commitment to doing the right thing.

That mindset has shaped every stage of my career. Combined with my graduate education from the University of Denver and years of leading enterprise data transformation initiatives, it became the foundation for how I approach every client engagement.

SISU Data exists to help organizations simplify complexity, build modern data solutions, and create lasting competitive advantage. We don't just deliver technology — we build trusted partnerships, solve meaningful problems, and create systems that enable businesses to grow with confidence.

That's what SISU means to me. And that's the standard we bring to every client we serve.

SISU Data. Strong data. Strong decisions.
